Our Projects

We Have Completed Latest Projects

Water tank installation



We are involved in a project focused on enhancing water access for small-scale farmers through the installation of water tanks. This project aimed to tackle water scarcity, which is a critical barrier to agricultural productivity, particularly in arid and semi-arid regions. By installing large-capacity water tanks, farmers were able to harvest and store rainwater for irrigation, ensuring a more reliable water supply during dry seasons. The increased access to water led to improved crop yields, reduced food insecurity, and enhanced livelihoods for the farming communities. Additionally, the project emphasized sustainability through community training on water conservation and management, empowering farmers to optimize water use for long-term food production.

Donation of seedlings



We participated in a seedlings donation project aimed at boosting food security in underserved communities. The project involved distributing high-quality, drought-resistant seedlings to small-scale farmers, providing them with the resources to diversify and increase agricultural production. By focusing on crops suited to the local climate, the initiative helped ensure sustainable farming practices while improving the resilience of farmers to climate-related challenges. The donated seedlings enabled farmers to grow nutritious food crops, enhancing their families' food supply and contributing to the overall reduction of hunger in the community.

Financial support



These funds can be used to purchase essential food supplies, support agricultural development, and finance programs that empower local communities to grow their own food sustainably. Additionally, donations can support innovations in food distribution and nutrition education, helping to build resilience in vulnerable populations and reduce hunger in both the short and long term. Ultimately, targeted monetary contributions can play a critical role in eradicating hunger and achieving SDG 2.


Equiping locals with knowledge



We equip locals with the knowledge to achieve a zero hunger community by implementing educational programs focused on sustainable agriculture, nutrition, and food security. Through workshops and training sessions, we teach community members how to grow diverse, high-yield crops using environmentally friendly practices such as crop rotation, organic farming, and water conservation techniques. We also promote the importance of balanced diets and food preservation methods to reduce waste. By empowering locals with this knowledge, we aim to build self-sufficient communities that can produce enough nutritious food to meet their needs, ultimately moving towards a future free from hunger.

Food donation



This organisation involves collecting excess food from restaurants, supermarkets, farms, and households, then distributing it to food banks, shelters, and low-income communities. By working closely with local partners, we ensure that the donated food is safe, nutritious, and reaches the most vulnerable populations. This project not only provides immediate relief to those facing hunger but also raises awareness about food wastage and encourages more sustainable consumption practices. Through food donation programs, we create a community-based support system that helps bridge the gap between food surplus and scarcity, contributing to the global effort to eliminate hunger
